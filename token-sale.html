Why Pigeon?

Pigeon is a decentralized social media application with the underlying blockchain technology making it an immutable and privacy-oriented application. How can people benefit by using the app? Apart from the secure nature of the app, the more time you spend with the app more rewards you will be learning and earning.

How influencers can generate income?

Pigeon offers a simple and easy way to connect with your followers and bridging the gap to increasing the interactions with them, also getting paid for the contents you create.

Is Pigeon a paid application for its normal users ?

No, Pigeon will not be a paid chat application even in the future it will always have a free version with maximum features available to its users.

How can I register for the whitelist private sale ?

You can click on the register/ participate button on the token sale page and fill out the form with all necessary details, and you will receive an email with confirmation which qualifies you for the whitelist sale.

What’s the minimum and maximum buy for private sales?

The minimum buy is a $100 and the maximum buy is $10,000 which is capped at the private sales.

Is KYC mandatory for the purchase of tokens?

Yes, KYC (know your customer) will be mandatory for the participation, which will be a one-time process which will be simple.

Are there any lockup periods for the private sale tokens?

Yes, there is a lockup period of 5 months when 20% will be released every month from the day token is listed on the platform.

What blockchain is the native token based on?

It will be on Binance Smart Chain, and it will be a BEP20 token.

What’s the staking APY of token?

The staking APY of the token is 46% per annum.
